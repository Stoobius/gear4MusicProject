Instruments - Drums

Acoustic Drums

Drums and Percussion form one of our largest ranges at Gear4music, with many drum kits getting the seal of approval from some of the biggest names in drumming.

We have Lars Ulrich’s favorite acoustic kits, Tama Drums, as well as Zildjian Cymbals, loved by Travis Barker.

Drum Kits have made their mark in virtually every musical genre from rock to hip hop, jazz to reggae, making the Acoustic Drum Kit a hugely popular instrument.
