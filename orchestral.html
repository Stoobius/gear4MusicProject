Instruments - Orchestral

Strings

String Instruments are still as popular today as they ever have been.

At Gear4music we've worked hard to develop a range of string instruments that meet the needs of beginners and advancing players.

With violins, violas, cellos, double basses and harps available in sizes from 1/16 to 4/4, we can cater for string players of all ages and abilities.

Woodwind

Woodwind Instruments, are one of our specialties. We have many years’ experience in instrument development and retail.

You name it, we are likely to sell it. We'll probably even have it in stock and ready to ship!

Whether you're looking for something classic like a saxophone, clarinet or flute, or something a little different like bagpipes and recorders we can help.

Brass

Our Brass Instruments combine an extensive collection from some of the leading brands such as Yamaha and Bach and Elkhart with our own affordable ranges.

Based on our years of experience, we offer an extensive and interesting range of instruments including trumpets, euphoniums and trombones.
